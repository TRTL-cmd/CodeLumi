
Brand & Trademark Usage — CodeLumi

Owner: Tortol studios
Contact: Tortolcoin@gmail.com

1) Trademark notice

- Use the mark `CodeLumi` with ™ internally until registered. After registration use ®.
- Display short notice in `README.md` and product materials: "CodeLumi is a trademark of Tortol studios." 

2) Logo & usage (suggested)

- Keep a single master logo file (SVG/PNG) in `docs/branding/` and do not modify it.
- Provide minimal brand guidelines: color, spacing, and allowed/forbidden uses.

3) Distribution policy

- Do not allow third parties to sell, rebrand, or redistribute the product or character
  assets without a commercial license from Tortol studios.

5) Next steps for trademark filing

- Perform clearance search (USPTO TESS, EUIPO, WIPO). If clear, file a national application
  (e.g., USPTO) or use the Madrid Protocol for international coverage.
- Prepare specimens showing the mark in commerce (website screenshots, app store pages, website).
- Consult trademark counsel for class selection (software, downloadable applications, character services).
